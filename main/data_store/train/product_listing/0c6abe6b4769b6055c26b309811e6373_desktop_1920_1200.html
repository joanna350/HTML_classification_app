Giglio Online Fashion Store For Women & Men

Giglio.com is an Italian online fashion store for Women and Men which deals with luxury clothing brands and offers fast and safe shipping worldwide.
Giglio company owns six boutiques in Italy which allowed it to become a milestone for the Italian and International fashion companies, such as Giorgio Armani, Gucci, Valentino, Roberto Cavalli, Saint Laurent, Jimmy Choo, Bottega Veneta, Moschino, Roger Vivier, Fendi, Salvatore Ferragamo, Moncler, Tod's and many other Italian fashion brands.

Our business mission is to make our customers feel the wonderful sensation of seeing their expectations realized; for these reasons, we ship designer clothes online for free to many countries.

Giglio follows the trend for Women's Designer Fashion and the latest styles from Top Designers for a sophisticated look or a sporty-chic one.
Our online Fashion Store also offers the best proposals of Men's Designer Fashion for classy or casual looks.
